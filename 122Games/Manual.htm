PONG

Introduction

Pong is one of the most oldest and classical games.

Rules / Gameplay

A ball is moving through the field. You have to keep the ball inside the field by moving the paddle up and down with the joystick.
The more you hit the ball in the middle of the paddle, the slower it will go. During serving the ball, the ball will go slower (according to the settings).
You get one point for every time you hit the ball successfully.

Settings

The following settings are available:

Pong Settings
Setting Description Options
Score to win The amount of times the ball needs to be bounced back into the field. 3, 5, 10, 15, 20
Ball slower during serve When serving, the ball will go slower No, Yes

SNAKE

Introduction

Snake is a game where you have to move a snake over the field which is getting longer. You have to try to not hit yourself.

Rules / Gameplay

At time intervals, the time grows. You can use the joystick to move up, down, left or right trying to move the head of the snake in a direction which is free. Once you hit the body of the snake OR the sides of the field with the snake, the gme is over. You get points for the length of the snake.

Settings

Pong Settings
Setting Description Options
Initial Length Initial length of the snake. 5-10
Growth speed Speed of which the snake grows in length. 1-10 pixels per minute
Auto bend at walls When hitting the head of the snake to a wall, the head will automatically turn. Disabled, Dangerous Side, Safest Side, Random
